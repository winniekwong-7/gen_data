  1. How would you interpret the beta coefficient from this regression?

A linear regression is performed to test the association between genotype (rs1861) and the continuous phenotype. The regression coefficient for rs1861 is 0.96698 (p <2e-16), indicating that for each additional copy of the C allele, the expected value of the phenotype increases by 0.96698 units. The significant p-value suggests evidence of a positive association between this SNP and the phenotype in our sample.
